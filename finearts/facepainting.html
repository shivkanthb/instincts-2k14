Face Painting





Wanna show your painting skills ? Come and try this.



Rules and Guidelines:

  1. Only one participant is allowed to take part in the contest.

  2. Participants are required to bring their own colors.

  3. The time duration is 45 minutes.

  4. Participants are required to bring their own friends on whose face, the painting will be done.

  5. Competitors may not use masks over any part of the face.

  6. Prosthetic pieces that are glued or taped to the face (fake cuts, noses, etc.,) are not
    7. allowed.

  7. The Judge's decision will be final.
