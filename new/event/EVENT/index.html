    THE EVENT

    REELS OF FIRE !
    Reels of fire is the annual national level short film festival organized by SSN College of Engineering, Chennai , as a part of the cultural festival, INSTINCTS. With an objective to bring out the best cinematic talents, Reels of Fire is all set to be back, even bigger this time. Sticking to our tag line "KURUM PADA THIRUVIZHA", we bring you some of the best short films and illustrate how they have evolved as a new form of festive entertainment. With the likes of Gowtham Vasudev Menon, Balaji Shaktivel, Editor Antony, Balaji Mohan, Balaji Tharaneetharan , Vignesh(Poda Podi) gracing the occasion as judges during earlier editions, this is your gateway to rub shoulders with directors of your dreams ! So why wait? Start sending your entries right away.

  • Rules and Regulations

    The following rules muat be followed:
    • The maximum allowed duration of the short film is 15 minutes, INCLUDING credits. Films longer than the specified limit will be rejected.
    • contest is OPEN THEMED. Short films of any theme are appreciated.
    • Strictly, no vulgarity or obscenity is allowed in the content of the film.
    • Original background music is a must. We do not encourage use of copyrighted material in your film.
    • Films of all languages are accepted. But SUBTITLES are a must for non-Tamil entries.
    • Only films shot after January 2012 will be considered.
    • The Top 10 films will be screened on the day of the event.

  • Schedule

    Entries : until 9th March '14
    Contest : At Instincts '14
    Winner : On the Day of the Event!
